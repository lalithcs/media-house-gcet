About Media House

Est. 2025

Media House is the officially constituted Media and Public Relations club of GCET, serving as the central media arm of the college. We are a multi-disciplinary body of creative students dedicated to covering a wide array of fields including videography, content creation, graphic design, social media management, animation, digital marketing, and documentary filmmaking. Our mission is to showcase the vibrant life and achievements of our college community to the world.
