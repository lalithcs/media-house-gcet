About Media House

Media House is the official media and public relations club of Geethanjali College of Engineering and Technology (GCET).

Formed to serve as the central media and PR arm of the college, our mission is to operate as a multi-disciplinary body covering a wide range of creative and digital fields including photography, videography, graphic design, content creation, and social media management.

A dedicated committee of talented students plans and executes the club's activities, ensuring professional media coverage for all college events.
